Step 2: Document Analysis & Information Detection

LifeVault analyzes your documents and extracts key information

🏷️ Automatic Information Detection

The system identifies important details such as document name, document type, issue date, and expiration date.

📋 What We Extract

Document Name: The title/description of your document
Document Type: Category (ID, Certificate, Medical Record, etc.)
Document Number: Unique identifier (passport number, license number, etc.)
Issue Date: When the document was issued
Expiration Date: When the document expires

⚙️ Manual Adjustment

You can manually edit any detected information. LifeVault stores exactly what you provide for complete accuracy.

Step 4: Automatic Expiration Monitoring

Never miss a renewal date again

⏰ Continuous Date Tracking

LifeVault constantly monitors your documents' expiration dates and automatically updates their status.

📊 Document Status Classification

Each document is classified into one of four statuses:

✅

Valid

Document is current and expires more than 30 days from now

⚠️

Expiring Soon

Document expires within the next 30 days

❌

Expired

Document expiration date has already passed

🎯 Status Update Logic

30-Day Alert Threshold: When a document has 30 days or less remaining, LifeVault flags it as "Expiring Soon"
Automatic Calculations: The system calculates days remaining for each document
Real-Time Updates: Status updates continuously as the expiration date approaches

🔔
